FAQ

Can I replay after I lose?

No. Once your snail dies, your run is over.

How does playing on multiple devices work?

Your snail is tied to the device it was created on. Opening the game on a new device will start a brand-new snail, with the timer and currency reset to zero.

However, if your snail has already died on one device, opening the game on another device will immediately end that run as well.

Description

Golftacular! is mini‑golf… barely. It blends party‑game chaos and dumb fun with mini‑golf precision and strategy. Up to 4 players take turns as Tortillo, Chuck, Fin, Poop, Papa, Mama, Claude, and Frend, each aiming for the hole while unleashing delightful mayhem on anyone in the way.

Enjoy endless replayability across dozens of handcrafted courses and tons of possible item combos. Items flip the script: turn the map into your personal parkour playground or an obstacle‑filled nightmare for your opponents. Call in airstrikes, play rock‑paper‑scissors, give your ball wings, place portals… and that’s just the start.

Features

  • Items, Items, Items: Stack wild abilities for game‑breaking combos.
  • Ear Candy Radio: An original, bumping soundtrack by Baba @babamadethis.
  • Express Yourself: Pick your favorite weirdo from a colorful cast.
  • Play It Your Way: Keyboard & mouse or full controller support.
  • Pass & Play: Short on controllers? Share one and keep the chaos going.
  • Solo Puzzles: No friends tonight? Conquer tricky courses on your own.

History

Both Good formed in 2024 while building its debut, the baby-themed local MOBA Goo Goo Grapplers (released November 2024). Guided by an ethos of approachable games with surprising depth, the Brooklyn-based three-person team, supported by outside collaborators, has stepped into 3D with Golftacular!, a party mini-golf game about ridiculous items and strategic shots. The studio is developing Golftacular! for PC (Steam), with a public demo available now and participation planned for the October Steam Next Fest, targeting a November 2025 release.
